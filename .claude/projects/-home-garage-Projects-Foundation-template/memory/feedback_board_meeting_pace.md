---
name: Board meeting — one question at a time
description: Board interrogation rounds should ask ONE question total, not one per persona. Wait for answer before next question.
type: feedback
---

Board meetings should ask one question at a time during interrogation, not all five at once. The protocol says "present all five questions together" but the user wants a conversational pace — one question, wait, next question.

**Why:** Five questions at once is overwhelming and doesn't allow answers to inform follow-up questions.
**How to apply:** During Step 2 of board-meeting, pick the highest-priority question across all personas and ask just that one. Let subsequent questions be shaped by the answer.
